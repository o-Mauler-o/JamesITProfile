IT Profile

James Stathakis

I’m James Stathakis, and this is my TED Talk personal profile.
	I am 20 years old, born in Sydney, moved to Canberra when I was young, and remained there until I was an adult.
	When COVID hit in 2020, I moved down to Tasmania to be with my parents, where I have remained up to present. 
	I like sports, with my main preferred sport being AFL, but with an interest in Field Hockey. 
	I played both when I was young, but stopped playing AFL upon entering Year 11. 
	For around 14 years or so, I have been playing Video Games as my past-time and I have become something of a Gamer. 
	It is only natural that I would go one step further and pursue a further education in IT. 
	Friends and Family have always stated that I’ve had a knack for all things computers and IT, and I thought, why not actually make it official. 
	In 2016/17, I started a bit into Web-Design as a hobby, and although I learnt a few things, it never really went anywhere.
